Sarmale (Cabbage Rolls)

Ingredients

  • 1 large cabbage
  • 500g minced meat (pork or beef)
  • 100g rice
  • 2 onions
  • 1 carrot
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • Salt, pepper, thyme, and dill to taste
  • 200ml tomato sauce

Instructions

1. Boil the cabbage leaves until they are soft.

2. In a large bowl, mix the minced meat, rice, finely chopped onions, grated carrot, tomato paste, and spices.

3. Place a spoonful of the mixture onto each cabbage leaf and roll tightly.

4. In a large pot, layer the rolls and cover them with tomato sauce.

5. Simmer on low heat for 2-3 hours.

6. Serve hot with polenta and sour cream.

Ciorbă de burtă (Tripe Soup)

Ingredients

  • 1 kg tripe
  • 2 carrots
  • 1 onion
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 200ml sour cream
  • 2 tablespoons vinegar
  • Salt, pepper, and bay leaves to taste
  • Garlic

Instructions

1. Clean and boil the tripe in water with bay leaves, salt, and pepper until tender.

2. Grate the carrots and chop the onion, then sauté in a pan.

3. Add the sautéed vegetables to the pot with tripe.

4. In a bowl, mix the egg yolks with sour cream and add slowly to the soup, stirring constantly.

5. Add vinegar and crushed garlic to taste.

6. Serve hot with a side of sour cream and hot chili peppers.

Mititei (Grilled Sausages)

Ingredients

  • 500g ground beef
  • 250g ground pork
  • 250g ground lamb
  • 5 garlic cloves
  • 1 tablespoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon thyme
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup beef broth

Instructions

1. Mix all the meats together in a bowl.

2. Add finely chopped garlic, baking soda, thyme, paprika, salt, and pepper.

3. Gradually add the beef broth while mixing until the mixture is smooth.

4. Shape the mixture into small sausages and refrigerate for a few hours.

5. Grill the sausages over medium heat until they are well cooked.

6. Serve hot with mustard and fresh bread.

Cozonac (Sweet Bread)

Ingredients

  • 1kg flour
  • 500ml milk
  • 300g sugar
  • 200g butter
  • 4 eggs
  • 50g fresh yeast
  • Grated zest of 1 lemon
  • 100g nuts
  • 100g cocoa
  • 100g Turkish delight
  • Vanilla essence
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

1. Dissolve the yeast in warm milk with a tablespoon of sugar and let it rise.

2. Mix the flour, remaining sugar, eggs, melted butter, and lemon zest in a large bowl.

3. Gradually add the yeast mixture and knead until the dough is smooth and elastic.

4. Let the dough rise until it doubles in size.

5. Roll out the dough, spread with cocoa, nuts, and Turkish delight, and roll it up.

6. Place the rolled dough in a loaf pan and let it rise again.

7. Bake in a preheated oven at 180°C for about 45 minutes or until golden brown.

8. Allow to cool before slicing and serving.
