Traditional Dishes

Sarmale are a traditional Romanian dish consisting of cabbage rolls filled with a mixture of minced meat, rice, and spices. They are typically served with polenta and sour cream.

Ciorbă de burtă

Ciorbă de burtă is a rich and flavorful tripe soup, seasoned with vinegar and garlic. It's a popular choice for festive occasions and is often enjoyed with a side of sour cream and hot chili peppers.

Mititei

Mititei, or mici, are small grilled sausages made from a mixture of beef, lamb, and pork, seasoned with garlic, thyme, and other spices. They are typically served with mustard and fresh bread.

Cozonac

Cozonac is a traditional Romanian sweet bread, usually prepared for holidays like Christmas and Easter. It is filled with a mixture of nuts, cocoa, and Turkish delight.
